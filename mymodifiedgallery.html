Clematis

Clematis (KLEM-ah-tiss or CLEM-uh-tis) are a diverse group of flowering plants, mostly known for their stunning climbing vines.

Lavender

Lavender is a perennial herb known for its beautiful purple flowers, calming fragrance, and numerous uses.

Aster

A genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae (the daisy family), known for their star-shaped flowers with numerous petals radiating from a central disk.

Petunia

Petunias are a popular choice for gardeners due to their vibrant colors, long blooming period, and relatively low maintenance.
